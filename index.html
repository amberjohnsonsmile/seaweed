seaweed

Kinda feel like every nutrition bar tastes like candy?

Savory snacking is where it's at, especially when there's a whole lotta sustainable seaweed blended into the mix.

Say what?

Say yes! Seaweed is wildly healthy, containing ample vitamin K, calcium and even protein. When grown right, it requires no fresh water, pesticides or fertilizers. It just contains sweet, sweet sunlight and seawater, making seaweed great for the environment. We like to source ours from innovative U.S. coastal kelp farmers.

We know, seaweed usually tastes like fish. Umami doesn't.

Instead it tastes like a Tuscan sunrise in your mouth. We highlight seaweed's umami qualities, the same flavor that gives tomatoes, mushrooms, truffles and Parmesan their "je ne sais quoi" loveliness.

Sounds good AF, doesn't it?

Why seaweed?

The situation

A planet of nine billion requires food systems that are sustainable, healthful, and carbon neutral. Seaweed is a regenerative superfood that Americans are beginning to fall in love with due to its acquired, sometimes fishy taste.

The fix

Umami is part of the solution. We bring together enviromental responsibility, nutrition, and flavor that will blow your mind.
